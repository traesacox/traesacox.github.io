Target Audiance

The target audiance for the WhetherToGoOut websit will be residence of the Preston, Soda Springs and Fish Haven areas of Idaho. The site is specifically geared towards families and individuals who enjoy outdoor adventures and would like to make sure the weather will be acceptable for their outings.

Persona - Middle Aged Mother - Linda Jolly

Linda is a stay a home mother of five. She has a college degree in Special Education. Her family is busy with many activities during the week which keeps everyone very busy and leaves little family time. Linda is dedicated to creating family bond through outside recreation on a weekly basis. Whether it is gliding tranquilly through breathe taking calm water on the lake near Fish Haven or hiking, Linda relies on the weather forcast to help her plan sucessful adventures for her family.

Persona - Executive in a Technology Firm - Jeff Robinson

Jeff is an executive in a technology firm and has a high pay and high pressure job. He typically works over sixty hours a week and really loves his job. He has a wife and three children. He has earned is Bachelors degree and MBA. Jeff loves to recharge his batteries with outdoor activities such as skiing, hiking, and fishing. He enjoys spending time with his family outdoors as well. Due to the fast paced life that Jeff leads he need up to date weather information in order to successfully coordinate his recreational actiities and get the much deserved stress releif time that he craves.

Scenarios

  • Do I need snow tires or chain on my vehicle to be safe on my outing?
  • Should I pack snow clothes, rain gear, or sunscreen?
  • Is the weather so nice I should expect crowds at the normal recreation spots?
  • Is today a bad day to fly a kite because of possible lightening?
  • Will there be strong wind on the lake today?
  • What time is sunrise and will it be clear enough to enjoy is should I get up that early?
